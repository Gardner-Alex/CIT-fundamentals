My life story, in a nut shell.

Hi my name is Alexander Gardner, but most people call me Alex. I was born in Georgia on Moody Air Force Base. I only lived there for a year before my parents moved to Florida because my dad was in the military. He was transfered to McDill Air Force Base in the Tampa Bay area. I lived in the superbs of Tampa until I was eleven. After living in Florida for ten years we moved to Ramstein Germany.

Germany was fun. I lived there for five years and go to go to a lot of cool places in Europe. We went to Paris, Berlin, and even made it into Denmark. While living in Germany I also got to see snow for the first time. In Florida it was always too hot for snow so I was very excited to first time I got to experience snow for myself. After Germany we moved to Abilene Texas. I finished high school there and left for my mission in Pheonix Arizona. The mission was great and I loved being there. After I came back I went to college at BYUI where I am now currently at.
